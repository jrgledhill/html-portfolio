John's Favorite Action Movies

Here are 3 of my most favorite action movies

Band of Brothers

This is a great mini-series about a group of soldiers who fought during World War II. I enjoyed the story and the characters.

Gladiator

A fantastic movie about a Roman General who was betrayed, enslaved, and forced to fight in the Arenas of Rome as a Gladiator.

The Patriot

Peaceful farmer Benjamin Martin is driven to lead the Colonial Militia during the American Revolution when a sadistic British officer murders his son.
